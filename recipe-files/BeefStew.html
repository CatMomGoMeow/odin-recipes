Beef Stew

Description

This delicious beef stew does not require alot of ingredients. It is the perfect comfort food on an autumn day.

Ingredients

  • onion
  • beef cubes
  • carrot cubes
  • cooked tomato
  • salt
  • olive oil

Steps

  1. Sautee onion in olive oil until golden
  2. Add beef cubes, tomato, and carrots
  3. Add salt and other spices desired
  4. Cook until carrots are softened
