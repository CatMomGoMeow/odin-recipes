Description

A quick and easy recipe for when you have some overripe bananas.

Ingredients

  • 4 ripe bananas
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 2/3 cups flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/3 cup butter, softened
  • 1/4 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 Tbsp
  • 1 Tbsp vanilla

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
  2. Mix sugar and butter first
  3. Then add eggs and bananas
  4. Add all dry ingredients
  5. Add vanilla
  6. Blend for one minute
  7. Pour into pan
  8. Sprinkle with sugar and cinnamon, if desired
  9. Bake for 1 hour and 50 minutes
