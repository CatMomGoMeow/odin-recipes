Mashed Potatoes

Description

Great recipe for a home cooked meal

Ingredients

  • potatoes, peeled, and cut in chunks
  • butter
  • milk
  • egg
  • salt

Steps

  1. Boil potatoes in water with some salt
  2. Drain pototoes and mash
  3. Add butter and beat by hand
  4. Add more butter if too dry
  5. Add milk and beat until smooth
  6. Add egg YOLK and blend
  7. Return to stovetop and bring to a boil
